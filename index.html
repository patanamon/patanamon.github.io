About me

I'm a Thai student pursuing a doctoral degree at Software Design and Analysis Laboratory (SDLAB), Nara Institute of Science and Technology (NAIST), Japan. I use empirical software engineering techniques to study software development process and software quality. Particularly, my Ph.D. thesis is focused on studying the important characteristics of modern code review practices and providing guidelines for practitioners to achieve high quality of software systems.
